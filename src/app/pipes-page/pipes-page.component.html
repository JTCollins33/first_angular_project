Here is the pipes page

First item is piped value and second is normal value

  • UpperCasePipe: "{{upperCaseString | uppercase}}" vs "{{upperCaseString}}"
  • CurrencyPipe: "{{price | currency: 'USD'}}" vs "{{price}}"
  • JsonPipe: "{{randomObject | json}}" vs "{{randomObject}}
  • DatePipe: "{{today | date:'fullDate'}}" vs "{{today}}"
  • PercentPipe: "{{percentValue | percent}}" vs "{{percentValue}}"
  • Custom KebabCasePipe: "{{toKebabCaseString | kebabCase}}" vs "{{toKebabCaseString}}"
